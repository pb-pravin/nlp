Most people are not very good at remembering abstract information like numbers. But a few people are good at remembering them, because they have a good memory. A good memory can help them greatly in their daily life. Some people are not good at remembering people's names. One easy way to help remember people's name is to look at him and say his name. It seems strange, but it works. Try memorizing the order of a deck of cards. This may seem like a meaningless task, but it will help you find the best way to remember things. Take out many things. Look at them for 30 seconds. Take them away and write down all the things you can remember. Practicing doing this many times can help you remember things well. Memorize your favourite songs or poems until you can sing or say them to yourself without any help. Try to do this often. Besides, there are also some games that can help you improve your memory.
