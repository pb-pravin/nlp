Beijing, the capital of China, has a history of thousands of years. There are many. places of interests in Beijing for tourists to visit. If you like traditional Chinese buildings, the Summer Palace is a good choice. Many ancient buildings and temples are located there. If you are interested in the history of China, you can go to the Palace Museum. You will be surprised at the beauty of ancient vases, paintings and other things used by emperors and empresses. Of course, the Great Wall is on the "must-go" list. As the saying goes, "He who doesn't reach the Great Wall is not a true man." Tian'anmen Square is in the center of Beijing. Almost every tourist will go there to see soldiers raising the Chinese national flag. Before 7 every morning, the Square is crowded with people from different places in the world.
