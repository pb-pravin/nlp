The kiwi lives only in New Zealand. It is a very strange bird because it can not fly. The kiwi is the same size as a chicken. It has no wings or tails. It does not have any feathers like others birds. It has hair on its body. Each foot has four toes. Its beak is very long. A kiwi likes a lot of trees around it. It sleeps during the day because the sunlight hurts its eyes. It can smell things with its nose. It's the only bird in the world that can smell things. The kiwi's eggs are very big. There are only a few kiwis in New Zealand now. People never see them. The government says that people cannot kill kiwis. New Zealand want their kiwis to live. There is a picture of a kiwi on New Zealand money. People from New Zealand are sometimes called "kiwis".
