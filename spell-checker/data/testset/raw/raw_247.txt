Everybody wants to be healthy. Here are three pieces of advice for you to keep healthy. First, food is very important. There are many kinds of healthy food. You can have bananas, apples, oranges and tomatoes because fruit and vegetables are good for you.But do not eat too much chocolate or candy. They are not healthy food. Healthy food helps you grow healthily and makes you strong and happy. There is a saying, "An apple a day keeps the doctor away." Second, sports can also help you keep healthy. You will feel relaxed and comfortable after doing sports such as playing basketball, running or swimming. Sports can help you build your body. Third, good habits are helpful, too. Go to bed early, get up early and drink more water every day. Don't be lazy. Remember all these things and you will be healthy and happy.
