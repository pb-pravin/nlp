People in different countries have different hobbies around the world. For example, in America, people's favorite hobby is watching TV in their spare time. It is the main entertainment for Americans to watch TV at least one hour in a day. 96% of the Americans see watching TV as their first hobby. The second one is going to the cinema or doing some sports. Next,it is reading or gardening. Except these, fishing is also a very popular hobby. In England, it's quite different. People there like to go to the flea market . They like to go there to see if there is something new. In addition, Englishmen like drinking tea. Russians like reading very much. Wherever you go, you will see the people who are reading carefully. Germans like reading as well. According to a survey, Germans read 11 books on average in 2006.
