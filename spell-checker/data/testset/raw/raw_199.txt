Nacy is a young girl. She likes growing flowers and keeping fish. Every morning, she gets up early. The first thing for her to look at her flowers and fish. She likes them very much. When she is eighteen years old, she goes to college. She has to ask her mother to look after her flowers and fish. Her mother is a careless woman, so she can't grow flowers and keep fish well. Two weeks later the fish are dead, and then the flowers die. The mother has to write to her daughter to tell her about her flowers and fish. Nacy is very sorry to learn the news. She says nothing, she writes back to her mother with only the words,"Then, what bout my father?"
