If you are a high school student, are you looking forward to a big adventure? Are you willing to travel on your own? You may want to become an exchange student. Exchange students can live in a foreign country and learn its language and culture. Students go to a high school and live with a host family, often for six months to a year. These programs are very beneficial to students. They can learn a lot about a different culture. They can also learn to be more independent and feel more confident. Being an exchange student helps you make friends with different people. Besides, it can help improve your understanding of the world. Exchange students share their own culture with people in the new cuntry as well. Organizations such as Rotary International offer exchange programs between many countries around the world.It's a chance of a lifetime.
