Alice is 13 years old now. She usually gets up at six o'clock. And she has breakfast at seven o'clock. Twenty minutes later , she goes to school. She's never late for school. She likes going to school. The teachers say she is a good girl. But she has a bad problem at school. She can't get along well with her new teacher. She thinks the new teacher picks on her. She asks her mother about it. Her mother says she should spend time thinking about it. Her mother asks her, "Are you working hard enough?" Alice thinks about it. Then she has a talk with the teacher. The teacher says she is strict with everyone and it is good for them. Alice knows she should do better at school.
